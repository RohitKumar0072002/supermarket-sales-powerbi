## Supermarket Sales Performance Dashboard – Power BI

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes supermarket sales performance across branches, product lines, and payment modes. 
The focus was on identifying revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, and profit-related patterns to generate business insights.

## Business Questions Answered
Which months show revenue growth or decline?
How does Average Order Value impact total revenue?
Which product lines contribute most to revenue?
Which branch performs better?
What is the revenue distribution across payment modes?
Why can revenue remain stable while MoM growth declines?

## KPIs Tracked
Total Revenue
Average Order Value (AOV)
Total Transactions
Month-over-Month Growth
Total Quantity Sold

## Dashboard Highlights
✔ Revenue Trend (Monthly – All Branches)
✔ Revenue by Product Line
✔ Revenue by Branch
✔ Revenue by Payment Mode
✔ Interactive Filters (Branch, Month, Payment Mode)

## Key Business Insights
Revenue fluctuations indicate seasonal sales patterns.
AOV increase does not always compensate for declining transaction count.
Certain product lines generate high revenue but may require margin analysis.
Payment mode analysis helps understand customer preferences.
MoM growth decline suggests deeper operational or pricing investigation.
